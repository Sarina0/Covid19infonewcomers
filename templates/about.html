ABOUT US PAGE

Covid-19 Info Newcomers is work results of 4 students of VCC and an idea that came to one of the team member and discussed in the group. Covid-19 Info Newcomers it is the site that gathers all the travellers travel to abroad and once they come back to Canada they asked to fill up their information with sign up page. The purpose of the site is gather travel information and it goes to our database and providing the necessary information these travelers need. It is a one stop place designed for Canada Health tips for Covid-19 and government polices specialy for 14 days safe qurantine. With the data base of people we gather we can contact them if they need vaccine or if with any test showing positive results of Covid we can contact these travelers and update their health situation.
